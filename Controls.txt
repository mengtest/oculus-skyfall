Controls - Keyboard

Movement:    			WASD keys
Turn Left:   			Q
Turn Right:  			E
Activate glider/parachute: 	F
Jump:        			Space
Reset game:  			R
Switch glide item:		Tab


Controls - Xbox 360 Controller
The controls have been mapped for the 'Tattie Bogle' OSX driver for Xbox 360 controllers. On Windows / Linux they will probably have to be remapped.

Movement:	  	    	Left Stick
Turn Left/Right:  	    	Right Stick
Jump:				A
Activate glider/parachute: 	B
Reset Game:			BACK
Switch glide item: 		RT / LT
